How the library works

Two depths.
One topic.

Every prompt in this library comes in two forms. Same topic, two different rigor levels. Pick the depth that matches the time you have and the use you want.

The system mirrors how I actually use AI day to day: a quick Sketch to test the idea, then a Blueprint when the answer matters enough to slow down and earn it.

The Sketch

Fast first try.

Three lines of structure, the inputs you need, the rules that matter. A working answer in under 15 minutes.

The Blueprint

The real work.

Multi-pass methodology, structured roles, output rigor, self-checks. Slower. Sharper. Worth it.
